# Privacy

Last updated: 2026

Endevina'm collects as little as it possibly can. There is **no Endevina'm server** — the app
runs entirely on your device.

## What is stored, and where

- **Your Spotify Client ID** and **OAuth token** (obtained via PKCE) are stored **locally on
  your own device only**. They are never transmitted to the maintainer or any third party.
- **Playlist and track data** fetched from Spotify is held **in memory for the current game
  session** to run the game, and is not persisted long-term or compiled into any database.

## What is NOT collected

- No analytics, telemetry, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
- No personal data is sent to the maintainer or any server operated by Endevina'm.
- Endevina'm does not sell, share, or transfer any Spotify content or data to third parties.

## Disconnecting

You can disconnect Endevina'm from your Spotify account at any time by logging out within the
app and/or removing the app's access at
[your Spotify account apps page](https://www.spotify.com/account/apps/). On disconnect,
Endevina'm deletes the locally stored token and Client ID.

## Spotify's role

When you use Endevina'm you are also bound by Spotify's own
[Terms and Conditions of Use](https://www.spotify.com/legal/end-user-agreement/) and
[Privacy Policy](https://www.spotify.com/legal/privacy-policy/). Spotify is a third-party
beneficiary of this notice with respect to your use of the Spotify Web API.
